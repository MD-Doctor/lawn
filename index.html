What is Southern Smiles?


Southern Smiles is a lawn care company offering professional lawn care solutions. We specialize in lawn maintenance, floral care, and tree service. Landscape maintenance is the art of keeping a lawn healthy, clean, safe and attractive. We provide services which include grass cutting, shrub reshaping, hedge cutting, trimming and fertilizing. Our team is very knowledgable about determining the mix of plants and floral arrangements to complete your landscape. We treat and care for lawns as if they were our own. Southern Smiles focuses on quality care and outstanding customer service.
